Participant Chip — Design Spec

Two independent signals. One axis per visual property. Zero ambiguity.

Requirements
Voted signal — badge only
A charcoal ✓ badge appears at the bottom-left of the face when the participant has voted. No badge = not voted. No face color change. The badge is the only voted indicator.
Offline signal — opacity only
The entire chip (face + badge) fades to opacity: 0.4 when offline. No text label under the name. "offline" shown as a tooltip on hover only. No other visual change.
Opacity is state-agnostic
Online voted and online unvoted have identical opacity (full). Offline voted and offline unvoted have identical opacity (40%). The opacity axis belongs entirely to online/offline — not to voted/unvoted.
Face is always neutral
All four states share the same light gray face, same border, same size. No dark background on voted chips. The face communicates nothing on its own — only badge and opacity carry meaning.
Badge style
Charcoal #374151, white border 1.5px, 16 px circle. Matches the existing ×N miss badge — consistent monochrome system. Bottom-left corner (miss badge is bottom-right — no clash).
After reveal
The numeric vote value is shown inside the face (existing behaviour). The voted badge is not shown after cards are revealed — the value itself is sufficient.
